Gaurav Taneja : Biography

Gaurav Taneja better known as Flying Beast is an Indian Youtuber, Vlogger and nutritionist. He also works as a pilot for the past 9 years. Recently he came into the limelight when he raised the issue regarding the safety of pilots in Air Asia airlines after which he was fired from his job.

Currently, he runs three Youtube channels named “Flying Beast ; Vlogging Channel”,“Fit Muscle TV : Fitness Channel” and "Rasbhari Ke Papa : Gaming Channel". He uploads travel-related vlogs on the Flying Beast channel and fitness related videos on the Fit Muscle TV channel. In Rasbhari Ke Papa , he do streaming of GTA-5 , Fall guys and some popular games.

Gaurav Taneja : Childhood

Born on 9 July 1986, Gaurav Taneja’s age is 34 Years as of 2020. He was born and brought up in a middle-class family from Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, India. He follows the Hindu religion and holds an Indian nationality.

Gaurav Taneja Childhood Photo

He completed his early schooling from Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya, Ghazipur, Uttar Pradesh. After that, he enrolled himself at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, West Bengal in 2008 from where he completed his graduate course in Electrical engineering. Since childhood, he was inclined towards health and nutrition and during his college days, he was known for his bodybuilding from where he decided to pursue his career in the same field by providing tips to others through his YouTube channel.

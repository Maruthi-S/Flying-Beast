Gaurav taneja : Career

Gaurav started his career as a Youtuber who used to upload his bodybuilding tips and nutrition tips videos. He has two popular YouTube channels that are “Flying Beast” and “Fit Muscle TV”. He has an experience of more than 13 years in this field, since his college days he used to do bodybuilding and share his tips with others.

Flying Beast is a travel vlogging channel where Gaurav and his wife share their travel-related posts. In this channel, he also shares about his personal life with his fans and this is one of the unique and interesting vlogs you can find online. He has posted nearly 215 videos on this YouTube channel and has over 1.6 million subscribers in this channel.

Fit Muscle TV is his second vlog channel on which he shares useful tips for bodybuilding and nutrition values. People who are very fond of gyming and bodybuilding find his vlog very interesting as he is different from others and his tips and guidance proves to be very successful. He has posted over 270 videos on this YouTube channel and has over 1.2 million subscribers in this channel.

Apart from his online career Gaurav has also worked for “Air Asia” airlines and other domestic airlines as a pilot, recently he was fired from his job as he raised his voice in media and on social media about the irresponsible behavior of “Air Asia” company regarding the safety of their pilots and passengers. Which lead to a controversy.

"Air Asia" Controversy

Recently the Gaurav made a big statement, he stated that his airlines “Air Asia” is neglecting the safety measures which they have to take for the safety of their pilots, passengers, and the aircraft.

After his statement, the “Air Asia” airlines sacked him from his job and rejected all his allegations. But Gaurav fearlessly raised his voice on print media as well as on social media regarding the issue and gained huge support from others and his fans.

He also stated that his employer not allowed any pilot to take sick leave when they were ill and also that the airlines didn’t follow any guidelines issued by the Government during this Covid-19 situation. Now DGCA has decided to conduct an inquiry over the issue and will make the decision regarding the issue as soon as possible.
